Founded in 2023 by Nikki and Naomi Leonard, The Noodle Nook is a culinary haven where macaroni and cheese dreams come true. Sisters with a passion for pasta and cheese, they crafted a menu celebrating the artistry of mac and cheese, offering a delightful fusion of flavors in a cozy, nostalgic ambiance.
